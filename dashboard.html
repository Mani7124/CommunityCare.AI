CommunityCare.ai

Platform Architecture

CommunityCare.ai transforms passive civic reporting into an active, autonomous pipeline.

1. Spatial Ingestion & Deduplication

Users capture infrastructure issues. Our backend calculates Haversine distances. If an issue is within 20 meters of an existing active ticket, it aggregates as a "Community Validation" rather than flooding departments with duplicate reports.

2. Multimodal AI Analysis

The image is sent to Gemini 1.5 Flash. The AI acts as an expert inspector, autonomously identifying the category (e.g., Water Leak, Road Hazard) and assigning a 1-10 Severity Score without human intervention.

3. Agentic Dispatch & Bureaucratic Routing

Based on the AI's categorization, the system instantly drafts a formal, legally structured email containing the photographic evidence, exact GPS coordinates, and AI assessment, dispatching it directly to the responsible civic department.

4. Closed-Loop Accountability

When the department marks the issue as resolved, the loop remains open until the community physically verifies the repair. Falsified resolutions trigger automated email escalations to higher municipal authorities.
